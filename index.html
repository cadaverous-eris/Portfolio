About Me

My name is Stuart, and I'm a full stack web developer. I started out by learning Java over three years ago in school, and once I started coding, I was hooked. I developed my skills by helping the coders on my school's robotics team and by modifying the code of video games, the latter of which is still a hobby of mine today.

Since then, I've attended a web development boot camp at The George Washington University in DC and am now beginning my career as a full stack web developer. In my spare time, I enjoy creating websites for personal projects, designing and developing games, and learning about new computer science concepts and technologies.
